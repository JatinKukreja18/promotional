Terms and Conditions

SBI Card holder who is referring his/her friends will now is termed as “Referrer”. Person getting referred for SBI Credit Card will now be termed as “Referee”

  1. The offer of voucher worth Rs 500 would be extended to referrer upon issuance of a new SBI credit card to his/ her referee. The referee will receive the voucher worth Rs 500 on meeting spends criteria. The referee should have made a payment of Rs 500 & above within 90 days of account open to be eligible for the offer. The voucher of Rs 500 will be credited within 90 days of account open for such customers on best effort basis. The time line provided for effecting credit is only indicative of expected period and may vary from the actual date of credit -
  2. The referrer would get a voucher worth Rs 500 from 60 days of SBI credit card issuance to the referee.
  3. Voucher for the referrer is capped at for upto 50 referrals per individual in a calendar year
